About Pecunia

Pecunia is the ideal solution to keep you up to date with the currencies around the world. You will have a straightforward and effective experience, completely free of any kind of advertisement or distraction. After all, they say time is money.



Highlights:

  • Exchange rates for 168 currencies in more than 247 countries.
  • Follow the rates without unlocking your device, you can use either the widget for iPhone and iPad or the Apple Watch app.
  • Save your favorites currencies to check the exchange rates quickly and easily.
  • Calculate prices right on the app.
  • Find currencies by country.
